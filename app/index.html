About
Kinetara Studios

Kinetara Studios builds free juggling software — simulators, games, and training tools powered by siteswap notation.

We believe juggling deserves great software. Our apps are built for jugglers of every level, from someone learning their first cascade to advanced siteswap theorists exploring 7-ball patterns.

Published under the Jugglesmith brand. Based in Colorado.
