Privacy Policy

JuggleCraft 3D by Jugglesmith / Kinetara Studios
Last updated: March 2026

What Data We Collect

JuggleCraft 3D does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We have no servers and no accounts.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Advertising ID (device identifier for ad personalization)
  • Approximate location (IP-based, not GPS)
  • App usage data for ad relevance

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data Security

All network communication (ad requests) uses HTTPS encryption. No personal data is stored on external servers by us.
