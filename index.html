About
Kinetara Studios

Kinetara Studios builds interactive skill tools — simulators, games, and training apps that turn complex physical skills into something you can visualize, practice, and master.

We started with juggling because it sits at the intersection of math, physics, and performance art. Siteswap notation gives us a precise language for patterns, and our 3D engine brings that language to life.

Based in Colorado. Published under the Jugglesmith brand.
