PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Jugglesmith ("we", "us", "our") built Pattern Drop as a free, ad-supported app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information Collection

Pattern Drop does not require you to create an account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Game progress (scores, stars, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information about your device to serve relevant ads, including:

• Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
• IP address
• General location (country/region level)
• App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google according to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Children's Privacy

Pattern Drop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is compliant with COPPA requirements. Ad content served to users who have indicated they are under the age of consent will be non-personalized.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

• Google AdMob — advertising
• Google Play Services — app distribution

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers. All game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
